Charity or non-profit?
I want to help you.

Why?

In modren sociaty, the web is cruital to all buisness, regardless of sector.
Unlike large public sector companies that have large budgets to spend on web services, charities and non-profits don't have the money to spend though desperately require these services to reach out to people and potential partners who need them the most.
These services are important, designers and developers should feel a responsibility to help with these projects.

How?

Within web devlopment there are a range of tools that can be used to a charities advantage, the first step is to find out the needs and apply the most useful.
The four main areas that I want to focus on are:

  • Awareness and infomation - Helping to get the infomation they need about your project or cause. This could be a simple infomation site, blog or full knowlagebase.
  • Presence - Helping people get in contact with or find your project. Online and offline.
  • Blogging services/Content Management Systems - Helping you keep your online infomation up to date.
  • Bespoke Web/Mobile Applications - Some sevices require complex systems like databases or applications to keep things going, sometimes services like this can cut down a lot of time consuming tasks.
